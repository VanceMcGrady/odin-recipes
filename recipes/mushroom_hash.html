Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Paprika

Cooking Instructions

  1. First, add a tablespoon of olive oil to the pan and set it to medium-high heat.
  2. When the pan is hot, add the potatoes and onions and cook until almost tender.
  3. Next, add the mushrooms to the pan and cook until tender. Add the garlic.
  4. Crack eggs and put on top of the hash with the yolk still intact.
  5. Cover the pan and let cook for about 8 min. to allow egg to cook through.
  6. Turn off heat and allow to rest for three additional minutes.
  7. Enjoy m8.
